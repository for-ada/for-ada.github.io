Before you can submit we have a few guidelines and suggestions...

Content You can submit articles of any sort as long as they fall under one of the six categories we already have. Interviews are great too but you must first ask for our permission. UNDER NO CIRCUMSTANCE will you use our name to gain an interview without our permission!!

Original Work Only All work must be original and unpublished. Meaning it can not be found anywhere before you submit it to us. That includes

  • Any form of Social Media
  • Other Publications
  • Your own personal blog or website

Where to submit Send your work to us by email @ for4ada@gmail.com

  • Remember to include:
    • Name
    • Age (p.s. we only accept work from 10 year olds to 19 year olds)
    • Intro to your piece (5-9 sentences explaining your piece)
    • How you came across our website

    • BE YOU!! Remember to write content that is pleasing to you! We don't want to read an article that you thought we would like. The whole point of collaborating is so that we can showcase multiple personalities through our Newsletter.
