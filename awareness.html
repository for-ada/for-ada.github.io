Knowing What you Stand for

It's astonishing the facts that arise about women in male-dominated industries. In the tech industry men outnumber women 7 to 3 according to Forbes.com. It's even less unlikely to see a company have a female CEO. Why exactly has it become so difficult for women to become successful in these industries?

The Fight for Education

It is easy to take education for granted in the United States where it's a requirement. It is a different story in places such as Afghanistan. While here we may complain about how we have to sit through yet another boring lecture, the young girl in Afghanistan dreams of learning about subjects like science or math. How can we make the most of our education and learn from those girls struggling for their education in Afghanistan?

How to Cope with Failure

It can be really disheartening to work on something and put a lot of effort into it only for it to fail in the end. It's important however to realize that we will constantly face failures. This may sound terrible but it's a part of life and there are certain ways in which we can deal with failure. It consists of being resilient and learning, how can we practice these two skills to live a happier more positive life?
