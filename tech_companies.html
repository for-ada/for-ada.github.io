Crea7ive

Pia Celestino and her sister, Paula Celestino, co-founded Crea7ive, which provides clients with web design, branding, ecommerce and graphic design. Originally a website design company, the Celestinos have expanded their business since 2006. They have worked with Wendy's, Libratone, and several more businesses that you can check out in Crea7ive's portfolio.

Kairos

Kairos is a facial recognition company located in Miami, Florida.

Tap Shield

Tap Shield's app allows college students to communicate to safety officials on potential threats or suspicious activity occuring in the school campus.

Nomads

Nomads, an abbreviation for Native Mobile Application Development, is a computer software company that designs and creates applications for their clients. Additionally, their Nomad TV allows clients to have their own multi-screen video streaming service.

The LAB Miami

In Miami's Wynwood Arts District, the LAB Miami provides working space for entrepreneurs, startups and organizations that want to work or gather in a creative, open, and diverse environment.
